Our Protective Packaging

We use custom-designed, eco-friendly packaging that cradles each seedling securely, minimizing movement and protecting them from environmental stress during transit. Our innovative solutions ensure your plants arrive as healthy as they leave our nursery.

Tips for Receiving Your Order

  • Unpack Immediately: Open your package as soon as it arrives.
  • Inspect Plants: Check each seedling for any signs of stress or damage from transit.
  • Hydrate & Acclimate: Water your seedlings if the soil feels dry. Place them in a shaded, sheltered spot for a day or two to acclimate before planting.
  • Report Issues: If you notice significant damage, please contact our customer support within 24 hours of delivery with photos.
